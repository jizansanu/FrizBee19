1 About the Rooms

All available rooms have two beds. If you would like to share a room you will get a better price. When registering, you can enter the name of the person you want to share with. Otherwise we’ll pair you up at random with a roommate of the same gender if you choose a shared room.

2 Add an extra night

When registering you’ll have to option to add an extra night to your reservation, at self-cost, and checkout on Monday instead of Sunday (breakfast included). There is no organised dinner on Sunday.
